What is GDSC?

Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) are community groups for college and university students interested in Google developer technologies. In GDSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community. GDSC Philippines consists of a group of students from the Philippines whose aim is to bridge the gap between theory and practical application for Filipino student developers.

What is the goal of GSCS?

Bringing student together in building meaningful solutions for the community.

Vision

To lead a college-based community group for students who are interested in developer technologies and for students who aspire to be better developers.

To communicate with our members with utmost respect, integrity, accessibility, and dedication.

To implement innovative ways to bring out the potential of our members.

To facilitate the growth of our members by providing social and learning opportunities related to developer technologies with the help of events such as workshops, forums, and tutorials.

Mission

To be a distinguished community group in the local student developer communities.

To impact positive and enjoyable experiences that people can look back and appreciate.

To uphold the legacy of the club by entrusting its future to the next generation of leaders.
