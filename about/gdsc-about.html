What is GDSC PLM?

Google Developer Student Clubs PLM is a duly constituted organization of Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila that aims to build great solutions, transform communities, and connect Haribons with diverse backgrounds and different majors through technology.

A Google Developer Student Clubs chapter for Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila (University of the City of Manila).
